Milton "Kellen" Baker

Bio

I am a musician and educator from Nome, Alaska. I am a tubist and guitarist; I studied tuba in college, performing classical, jazz, and modern works. I am a guitarist, performing in various rock and folk styles. I perform extensively with my current project, called Granddad, and teach and compose.
